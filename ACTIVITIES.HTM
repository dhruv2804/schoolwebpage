ACTIVITIES
  • Certain competitions oraganised like Rakhi Making Competition,Singing Competition etc.

The students showcased their talents in various activities namely; Model Making, Do You Know, Interesting Facts, Portrait Making, Collage Making and many more activities.
  • Celebrations of every festival takes place in school.

  • Many awareness programs are also conducted in school.
  • CAPS(Carrer Awareness Program Sessions)are also conducted in school to help children know all kinds of careers
